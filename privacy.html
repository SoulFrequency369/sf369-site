Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Soul Frequency 369 ("we," "our," or "us") operates a spiritual and motivational content brand. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our content publishing tool and social media presence.

2. Information We Collect

Our content publishing tool is an internal tool used exclusively by our team. We do not collect personal information from the public. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information from viewers or followers.
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on this website.
  • We do not collect or store any data from social media platform users.
  • We do not process user-generated content.

3. Third-Party Platform APIs

Our tool connects to social media platform APIs (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) solely to publish our own original content to our own accounts. When using these APIs:

  • We only access our own account data and publishing capabilities.
  • We do not access, collect, or store any data from other users' accounts.
  • We store our own API credentials securely and do not share them with third parties.
  • OAuth tokens are used only for authenticating our own publishing actions.

4. Data Storage

The only data we store is:

  • Our own content files (videos, images, text) on our local systems.
  • Our own API credentials and tokens, stored securely in environment variables.
  • A history log of our own published content topics to avoid repetition.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Our tool interacts only with social media platform APIs for the sole purpose of publishing our own content.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
